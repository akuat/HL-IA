Erudite - Having or showing great knowledge or learning
Lethargic - Affected by lethargy; sluggish and apathetic
Mundane - Lacking interest or excitement; dull; Of this earthly world rather than a heavenly or spiritual one
Inundate - to overwhelm or to flood and cover with water
Lucid - Expressed clearly; easy to understand esp. in the intervals between periods of confusion or insanity
Satire - the use of irony, sarcasm, ridicule, or the like, in exposing, denouncing, or deriding vice, folly or a literary composition doing such
Moralistic - characterized or expressing concerns about morals (right behavior)
Cynical - distrustful of human sincerity or integrity; doubtful as to whether something will happen or is worthwhile
Succinct - (esp. of something written or spoken) Briefly and clearly expressed
Reproach - Address (someone) in such a way as to express disapproval or disappointment or the expression thereof
